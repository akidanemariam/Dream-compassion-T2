Principles

How we earn trust

Commitments that guide our outreach and stewardship.

Dignity-first storytelling

We avoid exploiting images or details that put people at risk or remove their agency.

Minimize harm

We reduce exposure of sensitive information, especially for survivors of violence or trafficking.

Plain-language updates

We aim to communicate needs and outcomes clearly to donors and partners.

Safeguarding

Safety and privacy matter

We protect privacy and safety—especially for vulnerable people.

Do no harm

We avoid sharing identifying details about recipients unless consent and safety are assured.

Partner vetting

We prefer trusted organizations with established practices for protecting vulnerable individuals.

Secure handling

We aim to limit access to sensitive communications and maintain basic operational security.

Donors

Our commitment to donors

We use contributions responsibly, communicate honestly, and respect donor intent when possible.

  • Use funds consistent with stated purpose and donor intent (when feasible)
  • Share updates about program activity and outcomes
  • Respect donor privacy and communication preferences
  • Provide a clear way to ask questions or raise concerns
