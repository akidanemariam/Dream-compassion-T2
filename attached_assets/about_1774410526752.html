Values

How we show up

The principles that guide our work.

Dignity

Respect for the people we serve—privacy, safety, and human value first.

Partnership

Work with trusted local organizations to increase reach and reduce duplication.

Equity

Prioritize vulnerable communities affected by conflict, poverty, and violence.

Accountability

Communicate clearly about needs, actions, and outcomes.
